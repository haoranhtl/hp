About Me

I am a Visiting Scholar at the Laboratory for Information and Decision Systems (LIDS) at MIT, specializing in smart grids, Machine Learning, Reinforcement Learning, and renewable integration.

I earned a PhD in Electrical, Computer, and Energy Engineering from Arizona State University (ASU), where I also taught and conducted postdoctoral research. My work has been published in IEEE Transactions on Power Systems and IEEE Transactions on Smart Grid, among others, and I have presented at leading conferences such as the NeurIPS, KDD, IEEE ICDM, etc.

You can reach me at haorandd [at] mit.edu.
